Professor Guoqi Xie

Department of Computer Science | Professor

Professor Guoqi Xie, Ph.D. supervisor, currently serves as the Vice Dean of the School of Information Science and Engineering at Hunan University, Director of the Key Laboratory of Embedded and Network Computing in Hunan Province, and Secretary General of the Embedded Systems Committee of the Chinese Computer Federation (CCF). He is the founder of the ZVM project (a next-generation Type 1.5 real-time operating system virtualization solution). In recent years, he has published over 30 CCF A-class and ESI highly cited papers as the first or corresponding author, and published two books. He has been recognized as one of the top 2% of scientists globally, included in the "Lifetime Scientific Influence" list. He has also been appointed as an editorial board member for three embedded systems SCI journals, namely JSA, JCSC, and MICPRO.
